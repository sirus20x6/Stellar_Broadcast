Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 16, 2026

Stellar Broadcast ("the App") is developed and published by Aaron Shelhamer, operating as Faustian Bargain. This policy describes how the App handles your information.

Data We Collect

The App does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. No account creation is required. No login is needed. The App functions fully offline.

Advertising

The free version of the App displays ads served by Google AdMob. AdMob may collect device advertising identifiers (IDFA on iOS, AAID on Android) and usage data for the purpose of serving and personalizing ads, in accordance with Google's Privacy Policy.

You can opt out of personalized advertising through your device settings:

  • iOS: Settings > Privacy & Security > Tracking
  • Android: Settings > Google > Ads > Opt out of Ads Personalization

The optional premium upgrade removes all advertising from the App.

Analytics

The App uses Firebase Crashlytics to collect anonymous, aggregated crash reports. These reports contain no personally identifiable information and are used solely to identify and fix bugs. The App also uses Google Analytics for Firebase to collect anonymous usage statistics.

Leaderboard

If you choose to submit a score to the global leaderboard, your chosen display name and score are transmitted to our server. No other data is collected. Leaderboard participation is optional.

Third-Party Services

  • Google AdMob — advertising
  • Firebase Crashlytics — crash reporting
  • Google Analytics for Firebase — anonymous usage analytics

Children's Privacy

The App is rated for all ages. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. Since the App collects no personal data, no special provisions are necessary.

Data Retention

All game data (progress, settings, legacy upgrades) is stored locally on your device. We do not have access to this data. Uninstalling the App removes all local data.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.

Contact
