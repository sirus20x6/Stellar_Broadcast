Privacy Policy — Stellar Broadcast

Last updated: March 19, 2026

FaustianBargain ("we", "us") built Stellar Broadcast as a free, ad-supported game. This page explains what data is collected and how it is used.

Data We Collect

We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal information. The app does not require account creation, login, or any personally identifiable information to function.

Third-Party Services

The app uses the following third-party services, each governed by their own privacy policies:

  • Google AdMob — serves advertisements. AdMob may collect device advertising identifiers (AAID on Android, IDFA on iOS) and usage data for ad personalization. See Google's Privacy Policy.
  • Firebase Analytics — collects anonymous, aggregated usage and crash data to help us improve app stability. No personally identifiable information is collected. See Firebase Privacy.

Advertising & Personalization

Ads are served by Google AdMob. You can opt out of personalized advertising through your device settings:

  • Android: Settings → Google → Ads → "Opt out of Ads Personalization"
  • iOS: Settings → Privacy → Tracking → disable "Allow Apps to Request to Track"

Offline Functionality

The core gameplay works entirely offline. Network access is used only for serving ads and transmitting anonymous analytics.

Children's Privacy

The app is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children. AdMob is configured to comply with applicable child safety regulations.

Data Retention

All game progress is stored locally on your device. We do not store any user data on external servers. Uninstalling the app removes all local data.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.
