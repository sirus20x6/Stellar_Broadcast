## Full Description

Humanity did not build one ark. It broadcast seed ships across the stars.

Earth is gone. In a final act of survival, humanity scattered colony vessels carrying frozen colonists, cultural archives, and the tools to begin again. The fate of the other seed ships is unknown. You guide the last one still crossing deep space, searching for a world where the mission can take root.

EXPLORE THE UNKNOWN
Each voyage is procedurally generated. No two runs are the same. Discover planets with unique atmospheres, resources, gravity, radiation, biodiversity, and more. Worlds can harbour deep oceans, volcanic ranges, megafauna, subspace anomalies, toxic spore clouds, or ancient alien ruins — over 42 distinct surface features shape every landing. Will you land on the first habitable world you find, or risk everything searching for better soil among the stars?

MAKE IMPOSSIBLE CHOICES
Encounter over 167 narrative events with 400+ branching choices — asteroid fields, alien signals, derelict ships with dead alien voyagers, automated repair stations that trade but never give, crew conflicts, and cascading system malfunctions. Barter probes for repairs, sacrifice your cultural archive for fuel, or decrypt an alien captain's dying logs for star charts to a better world. There are no perfect answers, only trade-offs. Some choices carry weighted random outcomes — the same decision might save your crew or doom them.

MANAGE SHIP SYSTEMS
Your ship degrades with every light-year traveled. Monitor 14 interconnected systems: hull integrity, navigation, cryopods, shields, culture archive, technology, constructors, landing gear, and six scanner subsystems. When systems fail, the colony's future narrows. Your choices also shape your colony's ideology across five axes — from pacifist to militarist, secular to theocratic.

SCAN AND EVALUATE WORLDS
Deploy your scanner array on each planet encounter. Watch the scan line reveal atmospheric composition, temperature, water coverage, gravity, radiation levels, and hidden anomalies — but readings can deceive. Spend probes to reveal the truth. Is this world good enough? Or does something better wait in the next system?

LAND AND FACE YOUR LEGACY
When you finally choose a world — or your ship forces the decision — an epilogue unfolds across 11 scoring categories based on your ship's condition, the planet's traits, and its surface features. Endings range from Golden Age to Extinction. Some broadcasts take root. Others fail in silence.

BUILD YOUR LEGACY
Legacy Points earned from each voyage carry over between runs. Unlock permanent upgrades: reinforced hulls, advanced scanners, warp navigation, and more. Each voyage makes the next one a little easier — but the galaxy never stops surprising you.

DAILY VOYAGES
Every day brings a new seeded voyage — the same galaxy for every player. Compare strategies and scores. Did you land too early, or push too far?
